**Articles of Incorporation**
 
OF

**Orion Cybernetics, Inc.**

---

**Article I: Name**

The name of the corporation is Orion Cybernetics, Inc.

---

**Article II: Duration**

The period of duration of the corporation is perpetual.

---

**Article III: Purpose**

This corporation is organized for the purposes of profit and has the authority to engage in any legal act or activity for which corporations may be organized under the General Corporation Law of Delaware other than the banking business, the insurance business and the trust company business. To that end, Orion Cybernetics, Inc.'s primary purposes include but are not limited to the creation, manufacture, distribution, and servicing of artificial intelligence and robotics technologies, as well as any associated software, hardware, and peripherals. 

---

**Article IV: Authorized Capital Stock**

The corporation shall be authorized to issue two classes of shares of stock to be designated respectively Common Stock, $0.001 par value per share, and Preferred Stock, $0.001 par value per share. Total authorized shares of Common and Preferred are 1,000,000,000 and 100,000,000 respectively.
