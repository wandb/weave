**ARTICLES OF INCORPORATION**

OF

**SYNERGY VIRTUAL ENTERPRISES, INC.**

We, the undersigned, being of lawful age, in order to form a corporation for the purposes hereinafter stated, under the provisions and subject to the requirements of the laws of the State of Delaware, do hereby certify as follows:

**ARTICLE I**

The name of the corporation (hereinafter called the "Corporation") is Synergy Virtual Enterprises, Inc.

**ARTICLE II**

The address of the Corporation's registered office in the State of Delaware is 2200 Park Blvd, Wilmington, DE 19802. The name of its registered agent at such address is Riverfront Corporate Services, Inc.

**ARTICLE III**

The nature of the business, or objects or purposes to be transacted, promoted or carried on, are:
To design, manufacture, and distribute innovative virtual reality technology and services for personal and commercial use.

**ARTICLE IV**

The Corporation shall have authority to issue a total of 5,000,000 shares of stock, with a par value of $0.01 per share.

**ARTICLE V**

The Corporation shall exist perpetually.

**ARTICLE VI**

The private property of the shareholders of the Corporation shall not be subject to the payment of corporate debts to any extent whatever.

**ARTICLE VII**

The management of the affairs of the Corporation is to be vested in a Board of Directors. The number of directors of the corporation is to be not less than three (3) nor more than seven (7) as fixed from time to time by the board of directors.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, we have made and subscribed to this certificate this 2nd day of February, 2022.

1. Steven Liu, Incorporator
2. Sarah Gray, Incorporator
3. Rafael Garcia, Incorporator
