**ARTICLES OF INCORPORATION**

OF

**OPTIMUM TECHNOLOGIES INC.**

__________________________________________________

The undersigned, desiring to form a corporation under the General Corporation Laws of the State of North Carolina, adopts the following Articles of Incorporation:

**ARTICLE I – NAME**

The name of the Corporation is Optimum Technologies Inc.

**ARTICLE II – PURPOSE**

The corporation is organized for the purpose of engaging in any lawful activity for which corporations may be incorporated under the General Corporation Law of North Carolina. 

**ARTICLE III – DURATION**

The duration of this corporation is classified as "perpetual".

**ARTICLE IV – REGISTERED OFFICE AND AGENT**

The street address of the registered office of the corporation is 1155 Innovation Way, Raleigh, North Carolina, 27609. The registered agent at this address is Julie L. Thompson.

**ARTICLE V - CAPITAL STOCK**

This corporation is authorized to issue two classes of shares of stock: 

1. **Common Stock**: The total number of shares of common stock this corporation is authorized to issue is fifty million (50,000,000) with a par value of $0.01 per share.
2.  **Preferred Stock**: The total number of shares of preferred stock this corporation is authorized to issue is five million (5,000,000), with a par value of $0.01 per share. 

**ARTICLE VI - DIRECTORS**

The management of this corporation shall be vested in a Board of Directors. At least one director is necessary to constitute the board.

**ARTICLE VII - INCORPORATOR**

The name and address of the incorporator are as follow:

- Name: George L. Evans
- Address: 2148 Laurel Street, Greensboro, North Carolina, 27405

**ARTICLE VIII - INDEMNIFICATION**

The corporation shall indemnify its directors, officers, employees, and agents to the fullest extent permitted by law.

**ARTICLE IX - AMENDMENTS**

These Articles of Incorporation may be amended at any time with the approval of a majority vote of the total voting of outstanding shares of the Corporation’s stock.
