**ARTICLES OF INCORPORATION**
---

OF

**VIRTUPLEX INC.**

```
Pursuant to the provisions of the A7.525N, General Corporation Law of the State of Delaware
```
---

---

**ARTICLE I**

The name of this corporation is VirtuPlex Inc.

---

**ARTICLE II**

The registered office of this corporation in the state of Delaware is located at 1042 Minters Chapel Road, Kent County, in the state of Delaware. The registered agent in charge thereof is located at 222 South Rocket Road, Camden, Dover.

---

**ARTICLE III**

This corporation is organized and is to be carried out exclusively for profit-making purposes. 

---

**ARTICLE IV**

The total number of shares that this corporation is authorized to issue is one million (1,000,000) shares, valued at $0.001 per share.

---

**ARTICLE V**

The name and address of the incorporator are as follows: James Preston Holman, residing at 4607 Spirit Drive, Wilmington, DE 19880.

---

**ARTICLE VI**

No director shall be personally liable to the corporation or any of its shareholders for damages for breach of fiduciary duty as a director, except for liability: (i) for acts or omissions that involve intentional misconduct or knowing and culpable violation of law; (ii) for acts or omissions that the director believes to be contrary to the best interests of the corporation or its shareholders or that show reckless disregard for the director's duty to the corporation or its shareholders in circumstances in which the director was aware of the serious risk.

---

**ARTICLE VII**

The corporation can make, alter, amend, and repeal the bylaws. The directors shall have the power to prescribe and adjust the duties of all officers, agents, and employees of the corporation. 

---

**ARTICLE VIII**

The corporation reserves the right to amend, alter, change or repeal any provision contained in these articles of incorporation, under the provisions of the General Corporation Law of the State of Delaware as from time to time amended. 

---

In witness whereof, VirtuPlex Inc. has caused these articles of incorporation to be signed by James Preston Holman, its duly authorized officer, on this first day of April, year 2023.
