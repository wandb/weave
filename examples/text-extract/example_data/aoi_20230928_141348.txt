**ARTICLES OF INCORPORATION**

OF 

**SINCETECH INNOVATIONS, INC.**

In compliance with the requirements of the Business Companies Act (The Act), the undersigned, being desirous of forming a corporation, herewith certify:

**ARTICLE I – NAME**

The name of the corporation is Sincetech Innovations, Inc.

**ARTICLE II – REGISTERED OFFICE**

The address of the initial registered office of the corporation is 
195AD Gateway Towers, Revolution Street, Symphony, Utah,39219.

**ARTICLE III – PURPOSE**

This corporation is organized for the purpose of engaging in any lawful activity under The Act. Primarily, the corporation will be involved in the invention, development, and commercialization of advanced technology systems.

**ARTICLE IV – CAPITAL STOCK**

The corporation shall have the authority to issue, in total, 10,000,000 shares of capital stock, divided as follows:

a. 5,000,000 shares of Common Stock with $0.01 par value per share. 
b. 5,000,000 shares of Preferred Stock with $1.00 par value per share. 

**ARTICLE V – BOARD OF DIRECTORS**

The exact number of directors shall be fixated in the Corporation's Bylaws. This number may also be changed thereunder.

**ARTICLE VI – INCORPORATOR**

The name and address of the sole incorporator involved in this structure are as follows:

Alexander Thorn, 274-L, Northstone Avenue, Symphony, Utah, 392021

**ARTICLE VII - INDEMNIFICATION**

The Corporation shall indemnify, to the fullest extent permitted by the Business Companies Act, persons who perform services for the Corporation at its request as Directors, Officers, Employees, Agents, or otherwise, against expenses, judgments, fines, settlements, including attorneys' fees and related costs.

**ARTICLE VIII – AMENDMENTS**

Amendments to these Articles of Incorporation may be proposed by the Board of Directors or by the holders of at least 10 percent of the corporation's voting shares, and must be approved by a majority of voting shares before they can take effect. 

**ARTICLE IX – DISSOLUTION**

Upon the dissolution of Sincetech Innovations, Inc. any remaining assets shall be distributed for one or more exempt purposes within the meaning of section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code.

In Witness Whereof, we have hereunto subscribed our names this 7th day of January, 2023.
