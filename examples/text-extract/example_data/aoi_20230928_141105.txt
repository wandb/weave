*ARTICLES OF INCORPORATION*

OF

*SOLAR EXPLORATIONS INC*

This document represents the Articles of Incorporation of Solar Explorations Inc., a corporation as per the laws and regulations put forth by the Commonwealth of Virginia.

ARTICLE I

- NAME

The name of the corporation is Solar Explorations Inc.

ARTICLE II

- PURPOSE 

The purpose of this Corporation is to engage in any lawful act or activity for which corporations can be organized under the General Corporation Law.

ARTICLE III 

- OFFICE

The initial registered office of the Corporation is located at 2742 Walnut Lane, Virginia Beach, VA 23451.

ARTICLE IV 

- SHARES

The corporation shall authorize the issuance of 1,000,000 shares of common stock, all with a face vale of $1, and 250,000 shares of preferred stock, with a face value of $3.

ARTICLE V 

- INCORPORATOR

The incorporator of the Corporation is as follows:

Mark Ambrose Steadman
3571 Grove Street, Fairfax, VA 22901

ARTICLE VI 

-  DIRECTORS 

The number of directors in the corporation shall be one (1) until further amended through the by-laws. The initial director of the Corporation is Mark Ambrose Steadman.

ARTICLE VII 

- LIABILITY

Directors of the Corporation will have no personal liability to the Company or its shareholders for damages for any breach of duty in the role as directors. 

ARTICLE VIII 

- INDEMNIFICATION

Any person who was or is a party or is threatened to be made a party to any threatened, pending or completed action, suit or proceeding shall be indemnified by the Corporation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the undersigned incorporator, has executed these Articles of Incorporation this 12th day of June 2022.
